Welcome to Garnish B&B

Welcome to our charming guesthouse, a historic landmark nestled in the heart of Cork. With a rich heritage dating back over 150 years, our guesthouse has been a cornerstone of the local community, serving generations of visitors and residents alike. Situated amidst lush greenery and just a stone's throw away from the renowned University College Cork (UCC) and the vibrant surrounding areas, our location offers the perfect blend of tranquility and convenience. Step into our welcoming atmosphere and experience the warmth of traditional Irish hospitality. Whether you're exploring the picturesque park nearby, immersing yourself in the academic atmosphere of UCC, or discovering the vibrant city center just minutes away, our guesthouse provides the ideal base for your Cork adventure. At our guesthouse, we pride ourselves on offering comfortable accommodations and attentive service at a very reasonable price. From cozy rooms adorned with charming decor to thoughtful amenities designed to enhance your stay, we strive to ensure that every aspect of your experience exceeds expectations. Discover the allure of Cork and make our guesthouse your home away from home. Experience the timeless charm, unbeatable location, and genuine hospitality that have made us a beloved destination for travelers seeking an authentic Irish experience. We look forward to welcoming you and creating unforgettable memories together.
